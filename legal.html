What this app is

Still Point is a contemplative practice app organized around seventy original illustrated figures. It is not a fortune-telling service, not medical or psychological advice, and not a substitute for human counsel. The figures visit; they do not predict.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID and managed by Apple. Monthly and yearly plans renew automatically until cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. The 7-day free trial converts to a paid subscription unless cancelled before it ends. Lifetime access is a one-time purchase that does not renew. You may cancel or change your plan at any time from Settings → Subscription, which opens Apple's subscription management.

Privacy

Still Point holds no account system. There is no email, no password, no login. Your visits, reflections, and preferences live on your device and sync privately to your other Apple devices through iCloud. There is no Still Point server. No analytics, no third-party tracking. See the Privacy section below for details.
