— Still Point —

A daily quiet.

Seventy original illustrated figures who visit, one at a time, with a single observation for the day.

It is not a fortune-telling app. The figures do not predict the future. They do not tell you what to do. They visit you, one at a time, and offer one kind of attention.

Each morning, one figure arrives. You sit with the painting, the name, and one of nine quiet observations — an hour, a kind of weather, what is heard there, what grows, what is asked of you, what is taught. Some figures arrive upright. Some arrive reversed. Both are honest.

When you ask, you may also draw — One Figure for a single visit, or The Three for what is true, what to attend to, what stays.

— What's inside —

  • A figure each morning. One figure visits. The full Field Guide entry — painting, name, hour, weather, what is heard, what grows, what this asks, what this teaches.
  • Seventy illustrated figures, five wings. Central, Water, Earth, Sky, Fire. Fourteen figures in each wing.
  • One Figure and The Three readings. Draw a single figure for a focused visit, or The Three for what is true, what to attend to, what stays.
  • A journal that keeps every visit. On your device, with notes you may attach. Pattern cards thread through the timeline.
  • A patterns engine. Single-draw observations across The Three, plus behavioral patterns over your last fortnight.
  • The Sunday Weekly Read. A quiet summary each Sunday morning — the figures who came, the wing concentrations, the through-lines of the week.

— Held privately —

Still Point holds no account system. There is no email, no password, no login.

Your visits, reflections, and preferences live on your device, synced privately to your other Apple devices through your iCloud account. Apple sees encrypted data only. Still Point never sees any of it — there is no Still Point server.

No analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs. The figures visit; the journal keeps; nothing leaves your devices.
